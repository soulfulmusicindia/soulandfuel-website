Two instincts, one camera.

The studio

I shoot spaces the way I shoot rivers — paying attention to where the light actually wants to go.

Soul & Fuel is a one-person photography and film studio working across three lanes: interior & architecture photography, travel films and corporate films. They look like different jobs. In practice they're the same instinct, pointed at different subjects — read the space, find the honest angle, then get out of the way.

The name is the whole philosophy. Soul is what a place or a journey actually feels like once you've stopped performing for the camera. FUEL is the discipline that gets you the shot anyway — the scouting, the timing, the gear, the deadline. Neither one works without the other.

Based in Bengaluru. Available across India and abroad for the right project.
