Workflow recap

  1. Fill in the project name, type, location and year above.
  2. Drop in your full-size photos — tick "Tall" on one or two standout portrait shots if you want them to span two rows in the grid, like in your reference layout.
  3. Pick which photo is the cover (used on the overview page).
  4. Download the ZIP, unzip it, and upload the resulting folder into images/projects/ in your site (alongside css, js, etc.) via GitHub's "Add file → Upload files."
  5. Copy the two code blocks above into js/projects-data.js and work-interior-architecture.html respectively, and upload those two updated files too.
